Game Names
    -Pacnic (Picnic)
    -TastyTwist 

Overview
Items appear on the outside and make their way around the screen spiralling toward the centre. The player
has to prevent the items from reaching the centre by collecting them. Collecting food items rewards the player
with points, the goal of the game is to get as many points as possible.

Gameplay
The player collects an item by drawing a line through it. The player can get bonus points by collecting 
multiple of the same item in one sweep.
